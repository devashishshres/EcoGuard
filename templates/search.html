About EcoGuard Analyzer

This tool analyzes corporate sustainability claims and compares them with external reports to identify potential greenwashing. The analysis evaluates:

  • Misleading sustainability claims
  • Vague sustainability statements
  • Contradictions between company claims and environmental reports
  • Public sentiment analysis
  • History of false advertising claims

Note: Analysis may take a few moments to complete as we gather and process data.
